General enquiries

CPL library is developed and maintained by Edward Smith, who is a Lecturer at Brunel University and visiting academic at Imperial College London. General comments and questions are best by email: send your message to info@cpl-library.org. If you would like to develop new coupled examples, new features or just help with CPL library then please get in touch.

Technical support

For now, please direct support requests to info@cpl-library.org or one of the developers below. We are academic researchers who are supporting this free software on the side. We have tried to ensure the software is fully tested, user friendly and as clearly documented as possible and we will do our best to help with any problems. However, please understand that it may take some time to respond.
