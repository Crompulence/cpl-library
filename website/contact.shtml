General enquiries

CPL library is developed and maintained by David Trevelyan and Edward Smith, who are currently based at Imperial College London. We're happy to help in any way we can. General comments and questions are best by email: send your message to info@cpl-library.org, and one of us will get back to you as soon as possible.

Technical support

Right now we're working on putting together (a) some more comprehensive documentation and (b) a users' forum. For now, please direct support requests to info@cpl-library.org or one of the developers below.
